
# 5-Ingredient Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar

- 1 cup butter, softened

- 2 large eggs

- 3 cups all-purpose flour

- 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips


## Directions

### Step 1
Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

### Step 2
Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffier in texture. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Gradually stir in flour until well combined; fold in chocolate chips.

### Step 3
Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 2 inches apart. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
